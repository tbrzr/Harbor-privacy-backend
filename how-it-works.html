How It Works

Privacy that works
automatically.

Here's exactly what Harbor Privacy does, how it works, and why it's different from other solutions.

The Problem

Your network is leaking data every second.

Every device in your home makes thousands of DNS requests every day. By default, every single one is visible to your ISP -- logged, analyzed, and potentially sold.

📡
ISP Tracking
Comcast, Verizon, and AT&T can see every domain you visit via plain-text DNS queries -- even in incognito mode.
📺
Smart Device Telemetry
Your TV, thermostat, and speakers constantly phone home with usage data -- even when you're not using them.
🎯
Ad Tracking
Ad networks build profiles from your browsing behavior across every website and app on every device in your home.
⚠️
Malware Domains
Malicious domains don't get blocked at the browser level -- they need to be stopped before they ever load.
What's Included

One device. Every device protected.

01
DNS-Level Ad & Tracker Blocking
Blocks ads, trackers, and telemetry at the DNS level -- before they reach any device. Works on every phone, TV, laptop, and smart home device automatically.
02
Encrypted DNS over HTTPS
All DNS queries are encrypted so your ISP cannot see what sites you visit. Uses DNS over HTTPS (DoH) with your personal client ID.
03
Recursive DNS with Unbound
Queries resolve directly from root servers -- no forwarding to Google or Cloudflare. No third party sees your complete query history.
04
Hagezi Threat Intelligence
Updated daily with known malware, phishing, and tracking domains. Blocks threats before they load.
05
Automatic Cloud Failover
If your home device goes offline, DNS automatically fails over to a cloud backup. Your protection never drops.
06
Personal Dashboard
Log in to see your blocked stats, add custom rules, toggle Family Safe, and manage blocked services per device.
How It Works

Three steps to a private network.

01
I come to you
I visit your home, set up a dedicated Raspberry Pi privacy device on your network, and configure everything from scratch. Takes about an hour.
02
Every device is protected
The device becomes your DNS resolver. Every phone, TV, laptop, and smart home device on your network is automatically protected -- no configuration needed on each device.
03
It runs itself
Blocklists update automatically. Cloud failover runs silently. Your protection never stops -- even when you're asleep.
