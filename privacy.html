Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Harbor Privacy ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Tim Brazer, based in Pembroke, Massachusetts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website at harborprivacy.com or purchase our services.

Information We Collect

Website contact form: When you submit a contact form on our website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any message you provide. This information is processed by Formspree and delivered to us via email. We use it only to respond to your inquiry.

Payment information: Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

Installed device data: The Raspberry Pi device installed on your network processes DNS queries locally. We do not collect, transmit, or store your DNS query logs. All query data stays on the device in your home.

Remote access: We may access your device remotely via Tailscale for support purposes only, and only with your knowledge and consent. We do not access your device without prior communication.

Customer records: We maintain basic customer records including name, phone number, address, and network configuration details. This information is stored in a private, encrypted GitHub repository and is used only for service delivery and support.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted via the contact form
  • To deliver and support the services you have purchased
  • To contact you regarding your service, support requests, or billing
  • To maintain records necessary for service continuity

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

DNS Query Data

For on-site installation customers, your DNS queries are processed by the device installed in your home. They are not transmitted to us or any third party.

For Harbor Remote customers, your DNS queries are processed by our cloud DNS server. We do not log DNS query data. The only information retained is what you provide at signup. Query logs are not stored unless you contact us for support. Any logs collected are deleted immediately after the issue is resolved.

Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions: retained until your inquiry is resolved
  • Customer records: retained for the duration of your service relationship and up to 2 years after service ends
  • Payment records: retained by Stripe per their policies
  • DNS query logs on your device: configurable by you; disabled by default

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@harborprivacy.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree — contact form processing (formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Oracle Cloud — backup DNS infrastructure (oracle.com/legal/privacy)
  • DuckDNS — dynamic DNS (duckdns.org)
  • Tailscale — secure remote access for support (tailscale.com/privacy-policy)
  • Umami — privacy-respecting website analytics. No cookies, no personal data, no cross-site tracking. Anonymous page view statistics only (umami.is/privacy)

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. No cookie consent banner is required.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
