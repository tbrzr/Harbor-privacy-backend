COMMON QUESTIONS
Will I be charged right away?
Stripe requires a card to start your trial but you will not be charged anything for 30 days. Cancel before then and you pay nothing.

You'll be asked if you want to continue at $5.99/mo. If you cancel before then, you won't be charged anything.
What is Harbor Privacy exactly?
It works like a filter on your home internet connection. Ads, trackers, and malware get blocked before they reach your devices — automatically, on everything.
Do I need to install anything?
On iPhone you tap one button to install a profile. On Android you paste a URL into the Intra app. On your router you enter one IP address. That's it.
Can I see what's being blocked?
Your dashboard shows how many queries were made and blocked today. We don't log which specific sites — that's the privacy promise.
