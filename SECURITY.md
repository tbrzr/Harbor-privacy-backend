# Security Policy

Harbor Privacy is a privacy and security company. We treat security reports as critical and respond quickly.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please email **security@harborprivacy.com** with:

- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (or a proof-of-concept)
- Affected component (repo, endpoint, service)
- Your name/handle if you'd like credit (optional)

Do **not** open public GitHub issues for security reports.

## What to expect

- **Acknowledgement** within 48 hours
- **Initial assessment** within 5 business days
- **Fix or mitigation timeline** communicated as soon as scope is clear
- **Credit** in release notes if you'd like it

## Scope

The following are in scope for responsible disclosure:

- `harborprivacy.com` and all subdomains (`dashboard`, `booking`, `fax`, `adblock`, `stats`, etc.)
- `brazer.us` and subdomains operated by Harbor Privacy
- Source in this repository and other public `tbrzr/Harbor-privacy-*` repos
- Customer-facing APIs and authentication flows

Out of scope:

- Findings already publicly disclosed
- Denial-of-service against production
- Social engineering of employees or customers
- Physical attacks
- Findings exclusively against third-party services (Stripe, Resend, Telnyx, Cloudflare, AdGuard Home)

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

- Make a good-faith effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, or service disruption
- Only interact with their own accounts or test accounts they own
- Give us reasonable time to resolve before disclosing publicly

## Bounty

We do not currently run a paid bounty program. We're happy to publicly thank you and link your handle/site in our release notes.
