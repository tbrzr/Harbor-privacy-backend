COMMON QUESTIONS
Does it work on my kid's phone?
Yes. On iPhone you install a profile with one tap. On Android you enter a hostname in the built-in Private DNS setting — no app needed. The filtering applies immediately.
Can my child remove the protection?
On iPhone the profile is locked to your Screen Time passcode — they can't remove it without your PIN. On Android you can lock DNS settings with Digital Wellbeing parental controls.
What is Harbor Kids?
Harbor Kids creates a separate DNS profile for each child with stricter filtering. Their devices get extra adult content and malware blocking while your own devices stay on your standard settings.
Will I be charged right away?
Stripe requires a card to start your trial but you will not be charged anything for 30 days. Cancel before then and you pay nothing.
What is Harbor Light?
Harbor Light is our $1.99/mo plan with ad and tracker blocking on a single set of devices. No free trial, but it starts immediately. Harbor Remote ($5.99/mo) includes Harbor Kids, family profiles, and a 30-day free trial.
